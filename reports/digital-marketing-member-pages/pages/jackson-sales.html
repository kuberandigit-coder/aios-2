Product scope: 50 explicit Shopify Product IDs supplied directly (not a collection or CSV allocation) — only line items matching one of these IDs count toward these figures.
Report period: fixed calendar month, Europe/London timezone (DST-aware). Select a month tab above.
Jackson attribution: Jackson is SEO, same as Kamsi — Google Ads / Paid Search, Email, and Social orders are excluded entirely from every figure on this page; every other channel counts. Click a row's Sessions button in the Order Detail table to see every recorded session for that order.

Live month (July onward): current month is month-to-date; click Refresh to re-fetch the latest data. No automatic/background polling.
Privacy: no customer email, phone, address, or payment data is fetched, stored, or displayed.
