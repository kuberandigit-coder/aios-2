Notes & Methodology

Source of truth: Google Search Console Search Analytics API (service account, Restricted access, property sc-domain:ledsone.co.uk), fetched 2026-07-03. The company PostgreSQL GSC mirror (google_search_console.gsc_web_page) was checked read-only and matches the API exactly (sample: 70 clicks / 26,370 impressions on the top blog page) — the live API remains the single source of truth for this report.
Scope: URLs containing /collections/, /blogs/ or /blog/ only (1,385 pages: 1,148 collections, 237 blog pages). Product pages are excluded per requirement. Only pages with at least one impression in the month appear (GSC does not report zero-impression pages).
Month: June 2026 (2026-06-01 → 2026-06-30) — the most recent complete month; no partial current-month data used.
Target Keyword: the search query with the highest impressions for that page in the month (page+query GSC data). Pages with no query rows (very low/anonymised queries) show "—".
CTR (%): page-level monthly clicks ÷ impressions × 100, rounded to 2 decimals (e.g. 0.0185 → 1.85%). Flag (verbatim rule): CTR < 2% → Low CTR, otherwise OK.
Avg Position: GSC average position for the page over the month, 1 decimal.
