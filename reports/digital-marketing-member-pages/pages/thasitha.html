Overlap definition used: a SKU (Google Ads product_item_id) counts as overlapping when it has performance history (any date, any status) in Thasitha's PMax campaign(s) and at least one other ledsone.de campaign, per google_ads.product_performance. This is an all-time / any-status view, not a strictly "currently live" eligibility feed — no per-product current-assignment source exists for PMax campaigns in this database (confirmed via schema search 2026-07-15, documented in evidence/thasitha/2026-07-15_requirement-3-discovery.md). Paused/removed campaigns can still appear as an overlapping "Campaign 2/3" — treat this table as a historical + all-time conflict signal, not a live auction guarantee.
Campaign 1 is Thasitha's own campaign for that SKU (highest-cost one, if the SKU is in both her campaigns). Campaign 2/3/+more are every other campaign (any owner, any status) that has ever served that same SKU.
Baseline CPC = Campaign 1's aggregate CPC (total cost ÷ total clicks, all-time). Inflated CPC = the combined aggregate CPC of all other overlapping campaigns together (total cost ÷ total clicks across Campaign 2+).
Est. CPC Inflation = ((Inflated CPC ÷ Baseline CPC) − 1) × 100 — the standard formula. A positive value means the other overlapping campaigns' combined CPC is higher relative to Campaign 1's baseline; a negative value is shown as CPC Deflation.
Overlap Campaign-wise Data lists every campaign that has served the SKU (Campaign 1, 2, 3, and beyond) with that campaign's own Conversion Value, ROAS, Cost, and Conversions — all campaigns are always shown here, never truncated, even when more than three exist (see the expandable +more panel for full metrics).
Action recommends keeping the SKU in whichever campaign (among all listed, not just the first three shown) has the highest lifetime Conversion Value, tie-broken by ROAS, then conversions, then lower cost. Every one of the 328 overlapping SKUs has an Action — either a KEEP/REMOVE recommendation or "Business Review Required" when evidence is too thin to declare a winner (zero conversion value and at most one conversion). This is advisory only — recommendations are based on available product-level lifetime performance and require PPC review before any campaign changes. Nothing on this page is executed automatically.
Data source: PostgreSQL (read-only) google_ads.product_performance (all-time, all ledsone.de campaigns) joined to google_ads.campaigns, google_ads.merchant_products, and listings.shopify_listings (fallback). 301 of 328 overlapping SKUs (92%) resolved a product title/image — 238 via the Merchant Center feed, 63 more via the Shopify-listings fallback. The remaining 27 SKUs (8%) show the raw SKU ID with no image — no resolvable listing in either source.
