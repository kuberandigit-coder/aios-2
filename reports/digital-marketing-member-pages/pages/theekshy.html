Business Rules — Requirement 1 (source: requirement CSV 2026-07-15)

  • Rule 2 — ROAS Below Target (<3.0): ROAS < 3.0, rolling 14 days → 🔴 Critical → Lower tROAS; review bidding strategy
  • Rule 3 — ROAS Borderline (3.0–4.5): 3.0 ≤ ROAS < 4.5 → 🟡 Monitor → Hold budget; review again in 72 hrs
  • Rule 4 — ROAS Healthy (≥4.5): ROAS ≥ 4.5 → 🟢 Healthy → Maintain or scale budget 10–20%
  • Rule 5 — Post-Optimisation Improved: After ROAS > Before AND After Conv. Value > Before Conv. Value → 🟢 Healthy → Confirm; log as validated win
  • Rule 6 — Post-Optimisation No Improvement: After ROAS ≤ Before ROAS after 14 complete days → 🟡 Monitor → Revisit approach; try alternative fix
  • Next Review Date: Date Optimised + 14 days
  • ROAS: Conversion Value / Cost — N/A when Cost = 0
  • CTR: Clicks / Impressions × 100 — N/A when Impressions = 0
