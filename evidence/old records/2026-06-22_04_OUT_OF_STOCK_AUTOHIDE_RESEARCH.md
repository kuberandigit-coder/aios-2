**Date:** 2026-06-22
**Project:** Shopify Theme (DE / Ledsone)
**File:** `main-collection-product.liquid`

## What was explored
- Located `main-collection.liquid` / `main-collection-product.liquid`.
- Goal: when a product hits **0 stock**, hide it live from the collection and auto-fill its place with the next product (no gaps).
- Discussed feasibility of an **email alert** to the account holder whenever any product goes out of stock.
